### Specifications

<f-divider></f-divider>

A spacer can grow horizontally or vertically depending on its parentâ€™s layout.

In a row layout a spacer grows vertically and takes the height of the parent component.

In a column layout a spacer grows horizontally and takes the width of the parent component.
